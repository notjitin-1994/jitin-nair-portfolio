# Design System - Jitin Nair Portfolio

## Colors (OKLCH)
- **Void (Background)**: `oklch(12.72% 0.01 285.82)` (Tinted dark neutral)
- **Midnight (Surface)**: `oklch(15.72% 0.01 285.82)`
- **Cyan (AI Accent)**: `oklch(82.68% 0.16 225)` (Refined cyan)
- **Emerald (L&D Accent)**: `oklch(80.59% 0.15 160)` (Refined emerald)

## Typography
- **Headlines**: Playfair Display (Serif)
- **Body**: DM Sans (Sans-serif)
- **Technical/Mono**: JetBrains Mono

## Spacing & Rhythm
- Standardized grid-based spacing.
- Generous negative space for an "art gallery" feel.

## Components
- **Magnetic Cards**: Interactive, tactile feedback.
- **Count-up Stats**: Dynamic evidence of impact.
- **Portrait**: High-quality, smooth reveal.
