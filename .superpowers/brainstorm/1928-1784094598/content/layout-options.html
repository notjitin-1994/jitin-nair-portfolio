Scrollytelling Layout Options

How should the text interact with the 6-second video background?

A

The Split (1/3 vs 2/3)

Text blocks scroll vertically in a dedicated 1/3 column (e.g., left side) while the video occupies the remaining 2/3 and scrubs forward/backward based on scroll position.

B

Grid Intersections (Full Screen)

Video fills the entire screen. Text animates into specific focal points defined by the Rule of Thirds intersections (e.g., fading into bottom-left, then translating to top-right) as you scroll through the video frames.

C

Depth & Parallax (Z-Axis)

Video fills the screen. Instead of just scrolling up/down, text scales and fades out towards the viewer as new text emerges from the center, creating a 3D journey through the video.
