# Jitin Nair Portfolio - Product Context

## Users
Recruiters, founders, and engineering leaders looking for high-end AI systems architecture or L&D expertise.

## Brand
- **Tone**: Technical, architect-level, premium, minimalist yet detailed.
- **Principles**: 
  - Show, don't just tell.
  - Clarity in complexity.
  - Smooth, intentional transitions.
- **Anti-references**:
  - Generic SaaS templates.
  - Overly flashy, distracting animations.
  - Flat, uninspired layouts.

## Strategic Principles
- Dual-track expertise (AI & L&D) must be immediately clear.
- Proof of impact (stats) should be prominent.
- User journey should be frictionless.
