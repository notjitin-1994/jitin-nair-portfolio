# Product: Jitin Nair Portfolio

## Product Purpose
A high-end, senior-level portfolio showcasing expertise in Learning & Development (L&D), AI Systems Engineering, and Instructional Design. It aims to position Jitin Nair as a strategic leader and architect in industrialized learning.

## Target Users
- Enterprise L&D Leaders
- CTOs and HR Executives
- Strategic Talent Partners

## Strategic Principles
- Industrializing Learning: Pivot from "course creation" to "scalable content ecosystems."
- Engineering Precision: Treat L&D as a high-precision engineering discipline.
- Dual Fluency: Bridge the gap between learning science and AI infrastructure.
